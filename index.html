Every setting of Reverse ER2 was sampled and mapped into the plugin.

Impulse library

451 hardware captures

Each combination of Room Size (0.1–20.0) and Liveness (0–10) is represented as a 44.1 kHz stereo IR, using filenames like SPX90_ER2_REV_RS01_LV8_44p1k.wav.

DSP

Reverse space, tuned for guitars

Mono → stereo, wet-only pre-delay, convolution, then a gentle +1.5 dB high-shelf at 8 kHz to match the SPX90’s top-end. Custom dry/wet behavior keeps your direct signal intact before blooming into full reverse haze.

Controls

Simple layout, sweet spots baked in

Six knobs: Input Gain, Room Size, Liveness, Pre-delay, Mix, Output Gain. The mix curve is designed so that 0–25% is mostly dry, then ramps quickly into the “MBV wall of reverse” zone from 30–100%.

Requirements & formats.

Lightweight plugin with external IRs tucked inside the bundle.

  • Host – Any DAW that supports AU or VST3.
  • macOS – 10.13 High Sierra or later (Intel & Apple Silicon).
  • Sample rate – IRs captured at 44.1 kHz (host will resample as needed).
  • Installation – AU → /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/Components, VST3 → /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3.
  • IRs – Bundled in Contents/Resources/IRs/ inside each plugin.
